How do I check if my mobile phone is under warranty?
You can check your warranty status by visiting the manufacturer’s official website and entering your device’s IMEI number or serial number. This information is usually found in your phone settings or on the box.
Can I return or exchange my mobile phone after purchase?
Yes, most retailers allow returns or exchanges within 7–14 days of purchase, provided the phone is in original condition with all accessories. Check your seller’s return policy for exact terms.
What should I do if my mobile phone battery drains too quickly?
Try reducing screen brightness, disabling background apps, and turning off location services when not needed. If the issue persists, contact support to check for battery health or software updates.
