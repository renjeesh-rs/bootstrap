ZARA

Zara is a Spanish fashion retailer founded in 1975 by Amancio Ortega and Rosalía Mera. It's the flagship brand of the Inditex Group, headquartered in Arteixo, Spain. Known for its fast fashion model, Zara designs, manufactures, and distributes trendy clothing, accessories, and beauty products at lightning speed—often bringing new styles from concept to store in just a few weeks. With over 2,000 stores worldwide, Zara has become a global icon by reacting quickly to customer preferences and minimizing advertising, relying instead on store experience and rapid inventory turnover.
